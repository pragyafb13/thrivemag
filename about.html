About ThriveMag

We believe intentional living isn't a luxury — it's a practice. ThriveMag exists to give you the ideas, tools, and inspiration to build a life that actually fits.

Our Story

Born from a Simple Frustration

ThriveMag was founded in 2023 by a small team of writers and editors who were tired of the same two extremes in lifestyle media: either empty inspiration ("just believe in yourself!") or overwhelming, anxiety-inducing productivity porn ("wake up at 4am and hustle forever").

We wanted something in the middle — thoughtful, evidence-backed writing that helps real people make meaningful changes without overhauling their entire lives overnight.

What started as a newsletter with 200 subscribers has grown into a publication read by more than 500,000 people every month. Our readers are professionals in their 20s, 30s, and 40s who care deeply about health, work, money, and meaning — and want practical guidance to navigate all of it.

We cover five interconnected areas of life — Wellness, Career, Food, Mind, and Money — because we believe these things don't exist in silos. When your sleep is off, your focus suffers. When your finances are chaotic, everything feels harder. ThriveMag is where those connections get explored honestly.

What We Stand For

🔎

Evidence Over Hype

Every claim we make has to be backed by research, real experience, or both. We don't chase trends — we investigate them. If the science doesn't support it, we say so.

✍️

Depth Over Clickbait

We write long-form pieces because real change requires real understanding. You won't find listicles with empty advice here — just thoroughly researched, well-written articles.

🌟

Practical Over Aspirational

We respect your time and intelligence. Our articles give you actionable steps you can take this week, not vague lifestyle goals that require a complete personality transplant.

☤️

Honest About Tradeoffs

Living intentionally sometimes means making hard choices. We don't pretend you can have everything — we help you figure out what matters most and build toward that.

🌍

Inclusive by Default

Our audience is global and diverse. We write for people at different stages of life, with different incomes, bodies, and circumstances. We don't assume a default.

📖

Independent Voice

ThriveMag is reader-supported and editorially independent. We don't take money from brands to write favorably about their products. Our opinions are genuinely ours.
